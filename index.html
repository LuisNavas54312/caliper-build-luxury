01 — The Firm

Old-world craftsmanship, modern precision.

Caliper Build Partners LLC is a South Florida general contracting firm specializing in commercial and high-rise structural concrete, custom architectural homes, and luxury renovations. Founded by Luis Navas, we leverage extensive field experience at premier firms like L&R and KNM to deliver uncompromising precision — bridging old-world craftsmanship with modern efficiency. We take on a select number of projects each year, so every build gets the attention it demands.
